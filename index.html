Bio
Hi, I'm Ethan. I am currently a junior at the University of Chicago Laboratory Schools. This website was coded using HTML and CSS and outlines the academic and extra curricular aspects of my highschool career, including clubs, sports and community service.
Finance
The Finance Club at the Lab Schools educates students about the world of finance while also encouraging real investing using donations from Lab alumni and leaders. I have been a member of the club since joining sophomore year in 2014 and am currently one of seven members on the board of students.
Community Service
During the next three months, I will be working seven other students with the Honeycomb Project to lead and manage a service project for the homeless at La Casa Norte homeless shelter.
Baseball
I have participated on the Lab varsity baseball team since junior year. The team won Illinois State Sectionals in 2016 and is currently leading the 2A ISL division with a record 7-1.
Squash
I have participated on the Lab varsity squash team since junior year. Seeded within the range of 5th to 8th, I went 8-3 on the season, while the team went 6-5 for the 2017 season.
